Partnerships

Working with industry leaders to serve you better

Eyetopia Vision Insurance

We're proud to be in-network with Eyetopia Vision Insurance, making it easy for patients to use their vision benefits directly with us. Enjoy seamless access to quality eyewear and eye care while maximizing your coverage.

Essilor Labs

As partners with Essilor Labs, we provide access to some of the world's most advanced lens technologies. From Varilux® progressive lenses for seamless near-to-far vision to Crizal® anti-reflective coatings for glare-free clarity and Transitions® light intelligent lenses that adapt to changing light, Essilor helps us deliver unmatched comfort, protection, and performance.

Zeiss Labs

Through our collaboration with Zeiss Labs, we offer precision-crafted premium lenses, trusted by millions worldwide. From ZEISS SmartLife® lenses designed for today's digital lifestyle to ZEISS DriveSafe® lenses that enhance clarity and reduce glare while driving, our patients enjoy innovation, accuracy, and uncompromising optical quality.
